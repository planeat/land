HIVE — HIV Extractor

As a project for the Google Science Fair 2012, the system was aimed at developing a better predicting tool for the drug efficiency for HIV doctors by analyzing the data using the principles of non-transitive probabilistic comparison. Unfortunately, we couldn’t foresee the project further due to many factors.





MotherWorld — Strategic Game

A Strategic Android game to solve the levels representing various issues around the world. The game focused on spreading awareness and educating the users through the gameplay. Was selected among top 5 teams in AngelHack Hackathon Hyderabad in May 2016.





Huntme

A simple deer hunting game prototype developed using pygame. The gameplay is based on randomness.





Black Hole

A Two player game concept and rules that I came up with and working on the prototype. This is a strategy game which resembles a foosball game but with different concept and strategy.





Planeat

A platform for crowd synchronized health and diet plans. An on line MOOC for health and physical domains.
